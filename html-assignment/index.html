About Me

I was born in Nottingham, England on the 15th of November, 1996 and immigrated to Canada with my family sometime in 1998.
I went to SCHS and graduated in 2014 with an ICT SHSM diploma.
I studied Animation at Algonquin from September 2014 until December 2019 after dropping out of first year once and failing second year twice.

I love drawing and animating. I also enjoy playing video games and listening to music.
I live with my twin sister in a house previously owned by our late father in Kanata.
We own a fat, cream and white male cat we named Smough that we adopted from the OHS in January.
